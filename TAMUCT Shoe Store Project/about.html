About Us

Company Background

A&M Men’s Shoe Outlet will provide high quality men’s shoes at an affordable price. Our business is solely e-commerce based. Men’s shoes of various styles and sizes will be offered, ranging from casual sneakers to designer oxfords. We will offer shipping to United States and Canada. All shoes are made to order in New York City, NY using the highest quality, eco-friendly materials. We offer 24/7 customer service and fast shipping once orders are fulfilled.

Project Scope

Our highly motivated IT department will be planning, designing, creating, and implementing the online store. The expectations of the e-commerce site include functionality, usability, reliability, aesthetically pleasing design, easy-to-use graphical user interface, and simple ordering processing.
